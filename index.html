Welcome to Moomin Island's official website!

Moomin Island is my island in Animal Crossing: New Horizons, full of many fun attractions and cute animal villagers.

Things you can do while visiting Moomin Island:

  • Buy and sell at Nook's Cranny
  • Shop for clothes at the Able Sisters Tailor Shop
  • Visit the Museum and Town Hall
  • Visit and talk with island residents
  • Pick fruit and flowers
  • Stay at our luxury campsight
  • Get ideas for DIY's
  • Fish and catch bugs
  • Participate in the Moomin Turnip Stock Market
